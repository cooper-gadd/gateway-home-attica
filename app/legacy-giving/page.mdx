# Legacy Giving Opportunities

If you have already provided for Gateway Home with a planned gift, please contact Susan Herman, Office Manager at 585-708-4331.

**Gateway Home cannot provide legal or tax advice - please review any charitable gift arrangements with your legal and tax counsel.**

## Wills or Living Trusts

Name Gateway Home as a beneficiary with a specific amount, percentage, or property.

## Life Insurance

Name Gateway Home as the beneficiary/owner of your life insurance policy.

## Retirement Plans

Name Gateway Home as the beneficiary to avoid income tax on plan distribution.

## Charitable Remainder Trusts

Receive tax benefits while ensuring Gateway Home's future.
